Orcas are Social Animals

K44 horsing around with J47

Because orcas are social animals, they are usually found with members of their family (pods) and often times multiple pods are spotted together. We wanted to visualize how often the J, K, and L pods are spotted amongst themselves or with other pods. J Pod is spotted more frequently with just their own pod, whereas K and L Pods are more often spotted with other pods. Check out the charts below to see for yourself!
